The Global Gaming Championship (GGC) has unveiled its 2025 tournament structure, featuring an unprecedented $50 million total prize pool distributed across six major game titles. This marks the largest financial commitment in esports history.

## Tournament Structure

The championship will span three months, with regional qualifiers beginning in February 2025. The main event will take place in Las Vegas, Nevada, featuring:

- **Battle Arena** - $12 million prize pool
- **Velocity Racers** - $8 million prize pool
- **Strategic Warfare** - $10 million prize pool
- **Fantasy Legends** - $9 million prize pool
- **Cyber Tactics** - $7 million prize pool
- **Combat Zone** - $4 million prize pool

## Industry Impact

Tournament organizers expect over 10,000 professional players to compete in qualifiers worldwide. The event has already secured major sponsorships from tech giants and gaming hardware manufacturers.

"This represents a turning point for professional gaming," states GGC Commissioner Jennifer Park. "We're investing in the future of esports and recognizing the dedication of professional players globally."

Registration for regional qualifiers opens January 15, 2025.
